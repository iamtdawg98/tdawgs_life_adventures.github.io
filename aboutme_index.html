Why Did I Choose Coding?

For a long time, I have been extremely fascinated by video games and the power that they are able to command with their deeply entwining stories and the emotional effect they are able to have on people. Throughout my time in highschool and even to this day, the gaming community on YouTube is one that I actively watch and love being a part of. It never really crossed my mind that making them would be fun, and even when I was little I would think to myself "I don't even feel like that is a real job." The real moment I realized I was destined to become a Computer Science major was during a family movie night sometime when I was in high school. We watched a movie titled the Internship, where the two main characters end up becoming interns at Google. Instantly I fell in love with the idea of coding and was very captivated by the challenges they were presented. Coming out of that movie, I knew in my heart that I was destined to be a CS major and now that I have begun to learn coding, my decision has been strengthened 100%
