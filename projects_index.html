T Dawg's Life Adventures

T Dawg's Life Adventures (or TDLA) is my first major project as a rising computer science major. It is meant to be a way for me to practice and learn various web design tactics and languages (such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript). TDLA is also a place for me to form a platform and public presence for future projects and creations that the development team I am part of creates (such as a games or other software programs). Hopefully this website can help my skills to grow and flourish and eventually this site will provide a motivation for me to follow on this path and to create a plethora of new and innovative projects!
